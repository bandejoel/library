Welcome to the municipal library of the Bamenda city council

Located behind the Bamenda Grand Stand, along Commercial Avenue.
Placed under the authority of a Chief Librarian with rank and prerogative of a Chief of Service, assisted by support staffs. Their services include:

  • Librarians to help people find information they need
  • Borrowing books
  • registration and preparatory classes for TOEFl Exam
  • Free access to the internet.
  • Quiet study spaces for adults and children
  • play ground for children
  • Job, employment and career resources
  • And many more...
